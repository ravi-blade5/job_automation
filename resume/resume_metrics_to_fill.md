# Metrics To Finalize Before External Submission

Replace placeholders (`[X]`, `[X%]`, `[₹X Cr / $X Mn]`) in both variants with exact values:

1. Revenue influenced by AI product/partner initiatives.
2. Pilot-to-production conversion rate and baseline.
3. Active user or adoption numbers for AI-enabled workflows/products.
4. Time-to-value reduction percentage and baseline.
5. Cost reduction or effort savings percentage and baseline.
6. Number of enterprise accounts/workshops converted to opportunities.

Use one consistent metric style per bullet:

- `Outcome + unit + time period + brief method`
- Example pattern: `28% reduction in cycle time over two quarters by redesigning support triage with AI-assisted routing.`
