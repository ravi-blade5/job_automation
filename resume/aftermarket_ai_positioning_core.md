# Aftermarket Cloud + AI Force Positioning Core

## Purpose

Provide a reusable, interview-survivable story spine for positioning Ravi's HCL experience across three role lanes:

- AI Product Manager
- AI Solution Architect
- AI Consultant

## Core Story

- Worked on HCL Aftermarket Cloud, a modular aftermarket platform spanning dealer management, field service, parts, and remanufacturing workflows.
- Positioned and shaped AI Force capability infusion into the platform by identifying high-value AI intervention points across service, warranty, knowledge access, predictive maintenance, and remanufacturing workflows.
- Helped translate complex heavy-industry workflows into AI-enabled product and solution narratives that were credible for enterprise buyers and transformation stakeholders.

## Safe AI Intervention Themes

- Technician assist and knowledge search for field service workflows
- Predictive service and machine-health intelligence
- Warranty validation and anomaly / fraud detection
- Dealer and service workflow optimization
- AI-assisted parts / catalog lookup and service guidance
- Remanufacturing and salvage decision support

## Safe Language Rules

Prefer:

- defined
- shaped
- positioned
- translated
- identified
- designed
- structured
- enabled
- operationalized
- drove cross-functional alignment on

Use carefully:

- delivered
- implemented
- launched
- owned

Only use stronger delivery language where supported by evidence or where the bullet clearly refers to commercialization / external positioning rather than core engineering implementation.

## Resume Working Rule

- Product Manager version should lead with roadmap, GTM, customer discovery, prioritization, and business outcomes.
- Solution Architect version should lead with reference architecture, integrations, enterprise controls, and implementation patterns.
- Consultant version should lead with workshops, transformation narratives, use-case discovery, and stakeholder advisory.

## Guardrails

- Do not claim direct backlog, PRD, sprint, or engineering-team ownership unless Ravi can defend it.
- Do not convert platform metrics or customer-case metrics into sole personal impact claims.
- It is acceptable to say Ravi helped shape AI intervention strategy or AI-enabled use cases for Aftermarket Cloud if framed as product / solution / transformation work rather than hands-on model deployment ownership.
